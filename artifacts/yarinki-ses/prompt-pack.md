# Yarınki Ses — görsel üretim prompt kayıtları

Üretim aracı: built-in image generation
Use case: `illustration-story`
Metin politikası: bütün görseller sessiz; balon, SFX, logo ve yazı ayrı katmanda

## Ortak stil kilidi

Completely original clean modern digital romance webtoon art. Human-directed weekly webcomic finish, decisive dark blue-gray line art, flat local colors, one-step cel shading, selective soft atmosphere only on emotional accents. Natural asymmetry and restrained acting. Normal panels must be economical, not painterly. No imitation of any named artist or existing series.

Avoid: painterly concept art, photorealism, glossy 3D skin, fashion-ad posing, uniform cinematic render, excessive bloom, over-detailed fabric, copied Korean characters, existing comic panels, text, speech balloons, SFX, logo, watermark, malformed hands.

## Karakter model sheet prompt'u

Asset type: production character anchor sheet. Derya and Baran standing separately on a warm off-white neutral studio background. Show both characters in front, three-quarter, profile and back views, plus two restrained facial expressions each. Derya is 24 and 164 cm, warm light olive skin, narrow oval face, large almond dark-hazel eyes, straight brows with a tiny natural break in the outer third of her left brow, chin-length straight deep-espresso bob with one long right front strand tucked behind her ear. Faded mint crew-neck shirt, oversized charcoal zip hoodie, dark high-waist wide-leg jeans, plain white sneakers, cream canvas shoulder tote, tiny silver hoop earrings. Baran is 27 and 184 cm, lean, neutral light olive skin, long slightly narrow face, gray-green half-lidded eyes, thick straight brows, short naturally wavy black hair with a side part and exactly two characteristic strands falling toward his forehead. Clean white T-shirt, faded slate-blue overshirt with sleeves rolled twice, straight light-beige trousers, dark loafers, simple black analog watch, navy drawing tube. Clear height difference. Clean webtoon line economy, flat colors, one cel shadow. No labels or text.
